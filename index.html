Academic Program

Program Overview

The Associate in Aviation Electronics Technology (AAET) program is a 2-year course designed to produce competent avionics technicians. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on training in aircraft electrical systems, digital electronics, and avionics systems.

Career Opportunities

Aviation Industry Careers

AAET graduates are in high demand in various sectors of the aviation industry. Here are the top career paths:

Airlines

Philippine Airlines, Cebu Pacific, AirAsia - Avionics Technicians, Line Maintenance Technicians

MRO Companies

Lufthansa Technik, SIA Engineering - Aircraft Maintenance Specialists

Aviation Authorities

CAAP (Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines) - Aviation Safety Inspectors

General Aviation

Private aircraft maintenance, corporate aviation services
